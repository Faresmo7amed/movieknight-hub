Film Terms Glossary

Director's Cut
A version of a film edited according to the director's original vision, often different from the theatrical release.
Box Office
The total revenue a film earns from ticket sales.
Cameo
A brief appearance by a notable person, often playing themselves or a minor role.
Trope
A recurring theme, device, or convention used across many films.
Reboot
A new take on an existing franchise that discards prior continuity and starts fresh.
Franchise
A collection of related films sharing characters, settings, or a storyline.
Also used loosely to refer to the media brand as a whole, including merchandise and spin-offs.
Post-Credits Scene
A short scene shown after the credits roll, often hinting at a sequel or connected story.
Practical Effects
Visual effects created physically on set (models, makeup, animatronics) rather than digitally.
